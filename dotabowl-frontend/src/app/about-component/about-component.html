About

Dotabowl (the website) is a player stats and match-tracking hub for Dotabowl (the event).

How

Matches must be added manually during Dotabowl. Statistics are updated live, leaderboards refresh dynamically, and achievements are awarded as drainers compete. No "in-game" stats (KDA, hero picks, etc.) are loaded. Maybe in a future release...

Who

Dotabowl (the website) is built and maintained by hollahataballah. Dotabowl (the event) was built by the Bainbridge Boys and remains to this day the pinnacle of Dota gameplay, brotherly camraderie, and acute respiratory irritation.
